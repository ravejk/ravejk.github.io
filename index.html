Raven is a UX Strategist and Product Designer living in Brooklyn.

She currently works at The New York Times. Among her work there she launched Publisher's Award winning newsroom analytics tool Stela, a redesign of the Best Sellers list, NYTimes Podcasts, and helped evolve our first responsive framework (example: T Magazine). She's now working on special editorial projects, as well as research and experimentation around new product offerings.

Her past work includes a redesign of the Singaporean Government's online work permit process, a redesign of ICANN.org, map-based historical photo exploration tool OldSF.org, Pitney Bowes' Canadian mailing offerings, Priceline's post-purchase and account experience, and Amplify's K-2 lesson planning tools. She also taught UX at General Assembly and developed DesignLab's hands-on UX curriculum.
