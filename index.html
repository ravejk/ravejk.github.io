Raven is a designer, climber, MacGuyver and optimization junkie living in Brooklyn.

She currently works as a UX Strategist at The New York Times. There she's launched Publisher's Award winning newsroom analytics tool, Stela, a redesign of the Best Sellers lists, and a new presence for NYT Podcast series (like this one). She's now working on research and experimentation around new product offerings.

Her past work includes a redesign of the Singaporean Government's online work permit process, a redesign of ICANN's public comment tools, OldSF.org, Pitney Bowes' Canadian mailing offerings, Priceline's post-purchase and account experience, and Amplify's K-2 lesson planning tools.
